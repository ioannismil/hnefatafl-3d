Wizard's Chess — Rules

  • Board: 11x11. Light pieces defend the King at the centre. Dark pieces attack from all sides.
  • Turns: Dark moves first. Click a piece, then click a valid square.
  • Movement: All pieces slide any number of empty squares in a straight line (like a rook).
  • Capture: Sandwich an enemy between two of your pieces (custodial). The throne & corners count as hostile.
  • Shieldwall: A row of enemies on the board edge can be captured by bracketing both ends.
  • King wins by reaching any corner square.
  • Attackers win by surrounding the King on all 4 sides (3 if next to throne).
  • Camera: Middle-mouse drag to orbit, scroll to zoom.
